Agenda

JSConf 2009 will be a one track conference of some of the greatest thought leaders in the JavaScript community. The speakers and topics are presented in detail on the Speakers section. The current agenda for JSConf 2009 is presented below, speakers will be assigned to slots in the coming weeks as well as more details about the special social events. During all 20 minute breaks, there will be refreshments provided. Please check back often or follow our Twitter feed for updates as the schedule will not be finalized until March 15, 2009. We will be posting to Twitter as soon as new speakers, special events, and final agenda items are made public.
